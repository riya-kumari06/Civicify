AI Core

How AI Powers Civicfy

Three distinct AI systems work together to detect, prioritize, and resolve civic issues faster than any manual process.

image_search

Computer Vision — Image Verification

Our CV model analyses uploaded photos to verify the complaint is genuine, detect the issue type visually, and check for duplicate reports from the same location.

Accuracy: 94% · Duplicates blocked: 312 this month
psychology

NLP — Smart Categorization & Routing

Natural Language Processing reads complaint descriptions and voice transcripts to auto-assign the category and route issues to the correct government department or NGO.

Avg. routing time: 1.2 seconds · Departments reached: 8
crisis_alert

ML Urgency Scoring

A gradient-boosted ML model scores every report on a 0–100 urgency scale based on category, location risk, weather forecasts, and historical resolution time.

High urgency issues resolved 3.2× faster vs. manual triage
map

Predictive Hotspot Mapping

Clustering algorithms analyse historical complaint density to predict which areas are likely to develop issues — enabling authorities to act before problems escalate.

47 hotspots proactively fixed · 23% reduction in repeat issues

About Civicfy

Civicfy is an AI-powered civic engagement platform that empowers citizens to report, track, and drive resolution of local infrastructure and environmental issues.

Built for the Build-it ON hackathon by TCET EWT on the theme of AI for Social Impact & Sustainability, Civicfy uses NLP, Computer Vision, and predictive ML to make civic participation smarter, faster, and more transparent.

Every resolved issue is recorded immutably on blockchain, creating a verifiable public record of city improvements driven by citizen action.
